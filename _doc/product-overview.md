# MyToDo — Product Overview

## What It Is
MyToDo is a lightweight, personal task-management app. It gives individuals one focused place to capture every task that matters, attach due dates and priority levels, track progress, and filter between what's still active and what's already done — nothing more.

## Target User
Everyday individuals who need a simple, private to-do system. No teams, no enterprise workflows, no collaboration features. The primary persona is a single user who wants to stay on top of personal commitments without cognitive overhead.

## Positioning
Minimal, private, zero-friction personal task management.  
- **Not** a project-management or collaboration tool  
- **Not** an AI-powered planner or scheduler  
- **Not** account-based (no sign-up, no cloud sync overhead)  
Just tasks, due dates, priorities — private and instant from the first tap.

## Core Features
1. **Add Tasks** — capture title, due date, and priority level in one quick form, no friction.
2. **Due Dates** — attach a target date to every task so deadlines stay visible and nothing slips.
3. **Priorities** — High, Medium, or Low; always know what demands attention first.
4. **Mark as Done** — one-tap completion; list updates instantly.
5. **Filter Views** — toggle between Active and Completed to keep the working list focused.
6. **Personal & Private** — no accounts, no sync overhead; tasks are local and instant.

## Value Proposition
MyToDo removes the friction between intention and action. Due dates, priority tags, and active/completed filtering mean users spend energy *doing* — not remembering, searching, or second-guessing. The task list becomes a trusted system, not another source of anxiety.

## Key Messages
- "Your tasks. Your pace. Your way."
- "Everything you need. Nothing you don't."
- "Less mental overhead. More done."

## Scope Boundaries
- No account creation or authentication layer
- No collaboration, sharing, or team features
- No recurring tasks, reminders, or push notifications (MVP)
- No AI suggestions or automation (MVP)
