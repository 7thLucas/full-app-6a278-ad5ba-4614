# MyToDo — Product Overview

## Product
**Name:** MyToDo
**Type:** Personal task management web app
**Tagline:** Your tasks, your way — clear, simple, done.

## Users
Individuals — students, professionals, and anyone juggling daily responsibilities — who need a lightweight, focused tool to stay on top of personal tasks without the overhead of complex project management software.

## Positioning
MyToDo is a clean, no-friction to-do list app built for people who want to capture tasks fast, stay organized by priority and due date, and feel the satisfaction of marking things done. It sits squarely in the personal productivity space — more capable than a sticky note, less overwhelming than enterprise tools.

## Core Features
1. **Add Tasks** — Quickly create tasks with a title, an optional due date, and a priority level (High, Medium, Low).
2. **Due Dates** — Attach a target date to any task so nothing slips through the cracks.
3. **Priorities** — Categorize tasks as High, Medium, or Low priority to focus on what matters most.
4. **Mark as Done** — Check off completed tasks with a single tap, moving them from active to completed.
5. **Filter Views** — Switch instantly between Active tasks (outstanding work) and Completed tasks (done items) to keep the list focused.

## Brand & Tone
- Calm, focused, purposeful
- Clean visual design — airy spacing, clear hierarchy
- Encouraging without being gamified
- Primary color: indigo (trustworthy, focused)
- Accent color: emerald (completion, success, done-state)
- Neutral palette: slate grays for body copy and backgrounds

## Scope
Single-user, client-side web app. No accounts, no cross-device sync, no collaboration. Personal and private — tasks live in the app.

## Strategic Principles
- Zero friction to add a task
- Priority + due date make the list actionable, not just decorative
- Active / Completed split keeps the workspace clean and the completion loop rewarding
